About Me

Specializing in AR/VR, HCI and Haptics renderings. I am assistant professor in computing and Data Sciences, FLAME University, Pune, India. I have a diverse background from electronics enginnering to HCI including healthcare engineering. I am a passionate researcher and want to push the limits of virtual and augmented reality using wearablesa and haptics feedback. I have worked on various haptics projects: All type of tactile displays, Audio-tactile plugins, Virtual reality, Haptic gloves, navigation systems using vibrotactile displays, etc. I follow all the haptics conferences and Innovations in the the scientific community of hapticians. I have published a few papers in haptics journals and conferences and I hold a patent in haptics.
